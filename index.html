How are products chosen?

1
Must be independently lab-tested for adulteration, US/EU banned substances, heavy metals etc. Tests sourced from Unbox Health, The Liver Doc, Chirag Barjatya, Trustified, Food Pharmer & others.

Among products passing step 1, comparatively cheapest "one" in that category is selected.
3
After 1 & 2, that product must be used by some SIR Community members for at least 2 months.
4
Only then it gets listed above.
5
Zero brand influence, ads or paid placements for brands.
